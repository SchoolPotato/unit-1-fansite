Calvin and Hobbes

This is Calvin; he is the main character and one of two protagonists of Calvin and Hobbes. He is six years old, and the inventor of several machines, which include:
- A time machine.
- A thinking cap, which increases the user's IQ rapidly.
- A "transmogrifier," which can transform the user into any form.*
*There is also a hand-held version.

This is Hobbes; he is Calvin's imaginary friend and the secondary protagonist of Calvin and Hobbes. There isn't much interesting information about him; it's likely that he is six years old, as he is Calvin's imaginary friend.
